MULTIPLICATION OF NUMBERS WITH 9, 99 &amp; 999…

To multiply any number with 9,99 &amp; 999…

Step 1:- Write number of zeroes equal to number of 9s on the right hand side of the number to be multiplied

Step 2:- Now subtract the number to be multiplied from this number

For example:- 8*9

Step 1 :- Write one zero with 8 to make the number as 80

Step 2 :- Now subtract 8 from 80 i.e 80-8 = 72

So 8*9 = 72

LCM- Least Common Multiple

For e.g. 12= 12, 24, 36, 48, 60, 72, 84, 96…………

16= 16, 32, 48, 64, 80, 96…………

So there are lots of multiple of 12 and 16 which will be common but the least multiple which is common is 48.

So LCM = 48

HCF- Highest Common Factor

For e.g. 12= 1, 2, 3, 4, 6, 12

16= 1, 2, 4, 8, 16

So there three common factor=1, 2, 4 but the Greatest factor is 4. That is the HCF of 12 and 16.

Product of Two numbers = HCF x LCM

Questions in Test of Reasoning on Family/Blood Relationship are about the relationship of a particular person with another person of the family, based on the chain of relationships between other members of that family.

Family/Blood Relation Tests are an exercise to test the student’s ability to comprehend and come to the crux of an issue from complex, lengthy and even confusing data.

: A brother/sister to any of one's grandparents.

In-laws

In-laws only apply to brother, sister, and parents.

e.g. There is no relationship between you and your spouses cousins. My sister-in-law could be:

1. the sister of my spouse, or

2. the wife of my brother, or

UNCLE

: Uncle in American society this term can refer to a man in four different relative positions: father's brother; mother's brother; father's sister's husband; mother's sister's husband.

NEPHEW - NIECE:

Nephew or Niece is one who is the child of a sibling (or a half-sibling, or step-sibling, or a child of a spouse's sibling, or your spouse's sibling. Since the term derives from the Latin term, "Nepos" meaning grandson, it is possible an early colonial reference may have this meaning.
